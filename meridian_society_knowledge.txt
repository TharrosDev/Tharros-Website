SECTION 2: THE CORE PROBLEM THARROS SOLVES
================================================================================

PROBLEM 1 — MISSED CALLS = MISSED REVENUE (Status: CRITICAL_LOSS)
- Occurs: After business hours
- Reality: A lead that calls at 8pm and gets voicemail calls a competitor at 8:01pm.
- Impact: The business loses revenue while the owner sleeps.
- Who it affects: Any small business that relies on inbound inquiries.

PROBLEM 2 — REPETITIVE QUESTIONS (Status: RECURRING_DRAIN)
- Occurs: Every single day
- Reality: "What are your hours?" "Do you service my area?" "How much does it cost?"
- Impact: The owner's time is worth more than answering these questions on repeat.
- Who it affects: Businesses with common FAQs (trades, services, professional firms).

PROBLEM 3 — NEVER-ENDING ADMIN (Status: EFFICIENCY_GAP)
- Occurs: Hours every week
- Reality: Inquiry management, intake forms, follow-up emails — the paperwork of
  running a small business quietly eats the workweek.
- Impact: Owners spend more time on admin than revenue-generating activities.
- Who it affects: Any owner managing their own back office.

THE SMALL BUSINESS TRAP:
Business owners are pulled in too many directions. They're on the job site,
in client meetings, or off the clock — but customer inquiries don't wait.
Every unanswered call, forgotten email, or slow follow-up is a potential
lead lost to a competitor who WAS available.

SECTION 3: WHAT THARROS BUILDS — PRODUCT LINE
================================================================================

Tharros builds three primary types of Tailored, Autonomous AI Agents.
All agents are custom-engineered to solve high-impact operational gaps.

--- AGENT TYPE 1: THE CUSTOMER INQUIRY AGENT ---
Tagline: "Never let a question go unanswered."
Purpose: Handles inbound customer questions instantly, 24/7.
Channel: Website chat or messaging channels.
What it does:
  - Answers FAQs about services, pricing, availability, and location.
  - Understands natural language and local Ottawa context.
  - Escalates to the business owner when a situation requires human judgment.
  - Reduces phone and email volume by handling routine questions automatically.
Value delivered: Zero unanswered questions. Consistent, immediate responses.
Best for: Plumbers, HVAC technicians, Cleaning services, Landscapers.

--- AGENT TYPE 2: THE LEAD CAPTURE AGENT ---
Tagline: "Turn website visitors into qualified leads."
Purpose: Converts passive website traffic into qualified, actionable leads.
What it does:
  - Greets website visitors with a professional, branded opening.
  - Asks smart qualifying questions to separate high-intent leads from browsers.
  - Filters "tire-kickers" from serious customers automatically.
  - Collects contact information and coordinates follow-ups via email.
  - Gathers specific data the business owner needs (e.g., "What's your address?",
    "Is this an emergency?", "What's the square footage?") before any human
    involvement.
  - Operates while the owner is on the job or asleep.
Value delivered: Prevents "Lead Leak" — no visitor slips through without engagement.
Best for: Lawyers, Accountants, Consultants, Contractors.

--- AGENT TYPE 3: THE AFTER-HOURS INTAKE AGENT ---
Tagline: "Capture every lead, even when you're off the clock."
Purpose: Manages inbound communication when the business is closed.
What it does:
  - Handles all inbound messages received outside business hours.
  - Collects job details (type of work needed, location, timeline, urgency).
  - Assesses urgency level (emergency vs. standard request).
  - Compiles a clean, organized summary and delivers it to the owner each morning.
  - Ensures a "Zero Inbox" start to every workday.
  - Manages the "Off-Clock" business automatically.
Value delivered: No lead is lost overnight. Owner starts every day fully briefed.
Best for: Emergency trades (plumbers, electricians, HVAC), Property managers,
         Auto repair shops, Medical/dental clinics.

An AI agent for a boutique law firm has fundamentally different requirements than
one for a high-volume plumbing contractor. Generic subscriptions don't serve either
well. Our pricing is as custom as the agents we build — mapped strictly to the
value they create for your business.

PRICING IS DETERMINED BY THREE FACTORS:

1. SOLUTION COMPLEXITY:
   - Simple website concierges (single FAQ agent) are at the lower end.
   - Deep integrations with CRMs, booking systems, and business workflows

     industry knowledge require more build time.

   - Single channel (website chat only) is leaner.

   - Speak with the confidence of an expert.

   - Short, powerful sentences over long explanations.

3. MISSION-ORIENTED:
   - Every interaction moves toward a business objective.
   - Objectives: booking a consultation, answering a query, qualifying a lead.
   - Don't lose focus on the business outcome.

4. LOCAL AUTHENTICITY:
   - Reference Ottawa neighborhoods and local context naturally.
   - Sound like a neighbor who knows the market, not a call-center script.
   - Understand Ottawa-specific service contexts (e.g., winter HVAC demand,
     seasonal landscaping, Centretown professional services).

- Vague non-answers
